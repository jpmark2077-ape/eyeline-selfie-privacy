常见问题

无法使用相机或麦克风

打开 iPhone“设置”→“EyeLine Selfie”，确认相机和麦克风权限已开启，然后返回 App。

视频没有保存到相册

请允许 EyeLine Selfie 添加照片，并确认设备有足够的可用存储空间。

如何恢复 Pro 购买

在 App 的“关于”或 Pro 页面点击“恢复购买”，并使用最初购买时的 Apple ID。

免费录像在 30 秒停止

免费版支持最长 30 秒、720p 录像；Pro 可解锁不限时长和更高分辨率。

Frequently asked questions

Camera or microphone is unavailable

Open iPhone Settings, select EyeLine Selfie, enable Camera and Microphone access, then return to the app.

A video was not saved to Photos

Allow EyeLine Selfie to add photos and make sure your device has enough free storage.

How to restore Pro

Tap Restore Purchases from the About or Pro screen using the Apple ID that made the original purchase.

Free recording stops after 30 seconds

The free version supports up to 30 seconds at 720p. Pro unlocks unlimited recording and higher resolutions.

Still need help?

Include the steps, what happened, your iPhone model, and iOS version. Do not post private photos, videos, or purchase receipts.
