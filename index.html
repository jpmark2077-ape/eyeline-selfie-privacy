隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 12 日

简要说明：EyeLine Selfie 不收集、上传或出售你的个人信息。视频在你的设备上录制，并只在你选择保存时写入照片图库。

我们处理的信息

  • 相机：用于显示前置摄像头预览和录制视频。
  • 麦克风：用于在录制视频时录制声音。
  • 照片图库：仅在你选择保存时，将完成的视频写入照片图库。
  • 设备内设置：预览位置、提词器偏好和分辨率等设置仅保存在你的设备上。

不收集、不追踪

本 App 不会将视频、音频、提词文本、照片图库内容或设备内设置上传到服务器。我们不使用广告追踪、不建立用户画像，也不出售个人信息。

应用内购买

EyeLine Selfie Pro 的交易由 Apple App Store 和 StoreKit 处理。我们不会接触或保存你的付款卡信息；Apple 对其处理的数据适用其自身的隐私政策。

儿童隐私

本 App 不会明知收集任何人的个人信息，包括儿童的个人信息。

本政策的更新与联系

我们可能会在此页面更新本政策，并更新上方生效日期。如有疑问，请通过 App Store 的 App 支持入口联系我们。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

In short: EyeLine Selfie does not collect, upload, or sell your personal information. Videos are recorded on your device and saved to your photo library only when you choose to save them.

Information We Handle

  • Camera: used to display the front-camera preview and record videos.
  • Microphone: used to record audio with your videos.
  • Photo Library: used only to write a completed video to your photo library when you choose to save it.
  • On-device settings: such as preview placement, teleprompter preferences, and selected resolution, are stored only on your device.

No Collection or Tracking

The App does not upload your videos, audio, teleprompter text, photo-library content, or on-device settings to our servers. We do not use advertising tracking, build user profiles, or sell personal information.

In-App Purchases

Apple processes EyeLine Selfie Pro transactions through the App Store and StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment-card information. Apple’s handling of data is governed by its own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes and Contact

We may update this policy on this page and will update the effective date above. For questions, please contact us through the App Support link on the App Store.
